Setup Guide: How to Get Your Free AI API Key

Follow this simple guide to enjoy unlimited translations with TransPoint for free.

1 Get Google Gemini API Key (Recommended)

Google offers a very generous free tier for Gemini. It's fast and perfect for TransPoint.

  1. Go to Google AI Studio.
  2. Log in with your Google account.
  3. Click on the "Create API Key" button.
  4. Copy your key and paste it into TransPoint's Settings.

  1. Visit the OpenAI Dashboard.

Why do we ask for your key?

TransPoint is designed to be a "server-less" tool. This means we don't store your data or your money. By using your own API key, you get the highest speed and 100% privacy, while we keep the service running without expensive server costs.
