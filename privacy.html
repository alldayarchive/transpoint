Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 30, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to TransPoint. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our extension and website.

2. No API Key Storage

Your privacy is our priority. TransPoint DOES NOT collect, store, or transmit your AI API keys (e.g., Gemini, ChatGPT) to our servers.

  • API keys are stored locally on your own device (Local Storage).
  • They are used only to facilitate direct communication between your browser and the AI provider.

3. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data to ensure the service functions correctly:

  • Usage Data: We may use basic analytics to monitor traffic and improve user experience.
  • Billing Information: Payments are handled securely through Gumroad. We do not store your credit card details.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate several third-party services to provide our tool:

  • AI Providers: Google Gemini, OpenAI (requests are sent directly via your API key).
  • Payment: Gumroad (License verification).
  • Ads: Adstella (Anonymous ad tracking).
  • Analytics: Google Search Console.
